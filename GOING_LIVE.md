# Going live with WoofWalks

WoofWalks is positioned as a modern Mad Paws competitor with stronger aggregator UX: faster comparison, better filtering, cleaner booking, and clearer post-booking trust signals.

## Market context
- Customers want to compare multiple walkers quickly instead of messaging a directory
- Trust is won through ratings, review depth, live updates, and transparent pricing
- WoofWalks should feel like Uber for dog walking: browse, compare, book, track

## Key live integrations
- **alphinium-maps** — real GPS tracking, live route playback, walker location, and photo updates during walks
- **alphinium-payments** — booking checkout, saved cards, tipping, invoices, refunds, and payout flows for walkers
- **alphinium-push** — instant push notifications when a walk starts, photo updates arrive, and the walk ends

## Launch priorities
1. Real walker onboarding and verification
2. Live payments and payout compliance
3. Production-grade notifications and GPS permissions
4. Review collection after every completed walk
